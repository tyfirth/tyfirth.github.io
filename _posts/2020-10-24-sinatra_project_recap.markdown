My Sinatra project is a board game score keeping application that logs a match with a winner and date. 

The idea for the project comes from countless board game nights with my family over the years. Most game boxes have a piece of paper recording a game's match, who played and when, and most importantly, the winner. This application is intended to simplify keeping track of games for, ya know, bragging rights.

I've gone through a couple of iterations making this application and really, it isn't completed to my satisfaction yet. I started running out of time and I decided to simplify my application a bit to meet the project requirements. Now that I've met those, I would like to add some color to it. It functions just fine but I don't know enough about CSS at this point. I intend to go back to some of the previous bonus section to learn about that subject over the break. I would also like to add more functionality to the matches. Right now, it only records a winner but it makes sense for a match to also record the players. I overlooked this function after focusing on meeting the relationship requirements but I'll do some tinerking and hopefully have that added soon. 

I was really excited for this project. Sinatra and ActiveRecord are pretty cool and I'm happy to have built something my family and I can use on our next game night. Once I have it looking awesome and working how I'd like it to I would love to share it with others.
