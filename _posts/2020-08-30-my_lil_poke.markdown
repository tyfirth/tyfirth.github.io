I finally finished my CLI. Let me say that again. I. finished. my. CLI! Phew!

The concept of lil_poke is pretty straightforward. I wanted to keep this really simple (and it was still a bit of a challenge) since this is my first CLI. I struggled for awhile with ifferent ideas but felt myself limited by my own understanding of programming. I'm learning more and more how deep an ocean programming is and I've only dipped my tow in the water. So lil_poke is simply a lite Pokedex from one of my favorite pastimes. 

It provides a list of pokemon for a user to choose from and takes the user's selection. It then provides some basic stats on the chosen pokemon. 

Mindblowing stuff! It feels a little like the finger-painting of programming. I know the more I practice the better and more complex my programs will be. This was great practice though and helped me find the gaps in my understanding. Additionally, it really helped me solidify many concepts at the same times. 

I've seen other blogs that act as a journal whil the application is being built. I really like that but didn't consider it this time. Learning something new every day!
